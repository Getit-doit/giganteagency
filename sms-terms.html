Program Description

If you opt in, Gicante Agency LLC may send SMS messages related to your insurance inquiry and customer support. Message types may include: appointment scheduling, quote follow-ups, policy updates, billing reminders, and customer support. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply.

How to Opt In

  • Web form opt-in: You may opt in by checking the SMS consent box on our quote request form and submitting it.
  • Text-message opt-in: If offered in a conversation, you may opt in by explicitly requesting SMS updates and confirming your consent.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any message to opt out. You may receive a final confirmation message.

Privacy
